## Advertising Strategy Overview

JigMerge should advertise like a premium-feeling free product, not like a discount game portal. The strongest paid-media angle is not price - because the product is already free - but a combination of **novel mechanic + calm focus + instant access**. The campaign system should repeatedly communicate: `free browser play`, `no download`, `no signup`, and `a puzzle mechanic that feels different from standard jigsaws`.

The business model is ad-supported, so paid traffic quality matters more than vanity clicks. Campaigns should optimize for high-intent players who are likely to start a board, continue into more than one puzzle, and return later. That makes landing-page alignment critical: traffic should be sent primarily to `/play`, `/categories`, and selected blog articles only when the ad concept is explicitly educational.

The most efficient acquisition mix is: **Google Search** for active intent, **Meta** for scalable interest targeting and retargeting, **TikTok** for visual mechanic-led discovery, **X** for conversational niche targeting, and **LinkedIn** only for small tests around family-safe / focus-at-work / digital-wellbeing content. Creative should avoid loud arcade aesthetics and instead lean into satisfying tile motion, clean UI, and low-pressure brain-break positioning.

## Performance Benchmarks

Because JigMerge monetizes through ad-supported engagement rather than direct checkout, performance should be evaluated by **cost per qualified session**, **cost per play start**, **return-session rate**, and **pages per session**, not only by generic CPC.

| Platform | Primary KPI | Secondary KPI | Expected Test Benchmark |
|----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| Google Search | Cost per play-page visit | Play start rate | EUR0.40-EUR1.20 CPC |
| Meta | Cost per landing page view | Cost per play start | EUR0.20-EUR0.80 CPC |
| TikTok | Cost per landing page view | Hook rate / CTR | EUR0.15-EUR0.60 CPC |
| X | Cost per site visit | CTR | EUR0.25-EUR0.90 CPC |
| LinkedIn | Cost per content visit | Engagement rate | Higher CPC expected; keep tightly capped |

**Target Efficiency Metrics**
- Cost per play-page visit: EUR0.25-EUR1.20
- Cost per first play start: EUR0.80-EUR3.50
- Cost per return session: EUR1.00-EUR4.50
- Target blended ROAS proxy: 1.5:1 acceptable in early testing, 2.5:1 good, 4:1 excellent once return behavior is measured
